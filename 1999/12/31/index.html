Lemon Hydrolat

Botancial name: Citrus x limon

Plant part: Fruit peel

Origin: Portugal

 

Essential oil properties: Anti-anemic, antimicrobial, antirheumatic, anti-sclerotic, antiseptic, bactericidal, carminative, cicatrisant, depurative, diaphoretic, diuretic, febrifuge, haemostatic, hypotensive, insecticidal, rubefacient, tonic and vermifuge

Chemical profile: Limonene, a-pinene, a-terpinene, b-myrcene and sabinene
