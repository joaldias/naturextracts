Essential Oils

Essential oils are volatile compounds generally extracted by distillation processes. These volatile chemical compounds have different characteristics of fragrance, color and density, depending of the plant from which they are extracted.

The essential oils are very concentrated substances that are widely used in perfumery, pharmaceuticals, therapeutics and cosmetics for their well proven properties. Essential oils have long been known for their antiseptic, antiviral, antifungal, bactericidal, germicidal, healing, soothing properties, among others.

NatureXtracts essential oils are 100% pure and natural – they are carefully produced from selected raw materials and in accordance with the European Pharmacopoeia methods. Our essential oils are free from pesticides and heavy metals.
