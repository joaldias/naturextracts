R & D

NatureXtracts counts with Madebiotech’s knowledge-based ideas, experience and know-how to offer our customers R&D services.
NatureXtracts’ sister company - Madebiotech - provides consultancy services in scientific and technological areas like biotechnology, chemical engineering, environment as well as, energy and biomass valorisation.

Madebiotech designs and undertakes research for our customers in its fully-equipped lab, with guaranties of confidentiality and discretion. This solution allows for SME’s to innovate and adapt without major investment, targeting what really matters.

Toll Processing

NatureXtracts offers its clients expertise in toll processing service. The flexibility of our equipment makes possible to customize to fit your demands. This enables NatureXtracts to produce your exclusive essential oil, oligosaccharides or even your lipid or water extracts. Your raw material will be processed without the need for you to invest in equipment.

Benefit from NatureXtracts' expertise to develop your products from laboratory tests and pilot quantity to full scale toll processing.

TOLL PROCESSING TECHNOLOGIES
  • Supercritical CO2 extraction
  • Steam/ Hydro distillation
  • Water extraction
  • Fermentation
  • Cell growing
  • Lyophilization
  • Compounds purification by simulated moving bed (SMB) chromatography

NatureXtracts will be very pleased to process your raw materials and deliver you high quality tailor-made natural products!

Engineering Consulting

The lab-to-production step for new products or production lines requires some technical and scientific data that is not easy to obtain. Even if the prediction models are applicable, some parameters need to be accurate for the model to return consistent data. This is particularly important for processes involving Supercritical Fluids, Fermentation Technology, Essential Oils Production or Chromatographic separations and/or purifications.

Due to our accumulated knowledge and experience, our installed capacity and our equipment, we have all the necessary conditions to obtain technical and scientific data on these fields. In fact, we already proved to be effective in the development of new prototypes and industrial units.

If you have a product, a raw material and/or a process and if you need to develop your own production capacity, please contact us and we will be happy to assist you from the conceptual design up to the production of the first batch.
