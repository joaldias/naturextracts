State-of-the-Art Bioprocesses

Industrial extraction technologies designed to preserve the integrity of temperature-sensitive natural compounds.

Supercritical CO2 Extraction

Working at relatively low temperatures under total absence of oxygen, sCO2 extraction preserves the valuable active ingredients found in natural matrices. CO2 is food grade and classified as GRAS (Generally Recognized As Safe) by the FDA.

Extracts obtained are sterile, highly pure, completely free from residual solvents, and require no preservation agents — ideal for premium cosmetics, nutraceuticals and pharmaceuticals.

Steam & Hydro Distillation

Steam distillation rapidly removes aromatic molecules from biological matrices, preventing oil degradation by controlling steam temperature and pressure. Hydro distillation uses water to isolate essential oils and pure hydrolats from aromatic plants.

Lyophilization (Freeze Drying)

A water-removal dehydration technique operating close to vacuum, enabling water sublimation directly from the frozen state.

Ideal for temperature-sensitive biological products such as enzymes, vaccines, peptides and cell lysates — dramatically extending shelf life without chemical preservatives.

Bioreactor Fermentation

Employing bacteria and yeast cultures for targeted cell growth, exopolysaccharide production, enzymatic hydrolysis, and bioenergy derivatives — applicable across pharmaceutical, cosmetic, and food ingredient production.
