State-of-the-Art Bioprocesses

Our industrial extraction technologies ensure the preservation of temperature-sensitive biological compounds.

Supercritical CO2 Extraction

An innovative, high-pressure process working at low temperatures in the complete absence of oxygen. It preserves the integrity of active ingredients, creating sterile, highly pure extracts that are solvent-free and require no chemical preservation.

Our food-grade CO2 is classified as GRAS (Generally Recognized As Safe) by the FDA, making extracts perfect for premium cosmetics and nutraceuticals.

Lyophilization (Freeze Drying)

A water-removal dehydration technique that operates close to a vacuum, enabling sublimation of water molecules from frozen material.

Lyophilization extends shelf life and is the absolute best choice when preserving temperature-sensitive products like enzymes, proteins, and cell cultures of biological origin.

Steam & Hydro Distillation

Commonly used to extract high-purity essential oils and hydrolats. Controls steam temperature and flow rate to prevent thermal damage to aromatic molecules.

Bioreactor Fermentation

Equipped for target cell growth, production of exopolysaccharides, bioenergy derivatives, and enzymatic hydrolysis utilizing bacteria and yeast cultures.

Analytical Verification

Equipped with chromatography systems (HPLC / Gas Chromatography) and UV/VIS spectroscopy to analyze and verify extract purity and reproducibility.

NatureXtracts Custom Manufacturing

Promoting the concept of biomass biorefinery through low-impact extraction technologies and premium service.
